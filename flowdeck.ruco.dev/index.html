play and turn

Run a single card with flowdeck play <slug> or the full deck with flowdeck turn. Dependencies are declared, not guessed.

Deck lifecycle

Cards move from _stock → active column → _meld (shipped) or _discard. Frozen cards park in _frozen with an unfreeze signal.
